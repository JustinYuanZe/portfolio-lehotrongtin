About Me

I am Le Ho Trong Tin, a dedicated mathematics educator specializing in number theory and mathematical olympiad training. With a profound passion for the elegance and depth of number theory, I guide students through the fascinating world of integers, primes, congruences, and Diophantine equations.

My journey in mathematics began with national mathematical competitions, where I developed a deep appreciation for problem-solving and proof-based mathematics. This foundation has shaped my teaching philosophy, which emphasizes conceptual understanding, creative problem-solving, and mathematical rigor.

Specialized Focus Areas:

  • Elementary and Analytic Number Theory
  • Diophantine Equations and Congruences
  • Mathematical Olympiad Problem Solving
  • Competition Mathematics Training
  • Proof Techniques and Mathematical Writing

I work with students preparing for national and international mathematical competitions, helping them develop the problem-solving strategies and mathematical maturity needed to excel in these challenging contests. My approach combines deep theoretical understanding with extensive practice on carefully selected problems.
